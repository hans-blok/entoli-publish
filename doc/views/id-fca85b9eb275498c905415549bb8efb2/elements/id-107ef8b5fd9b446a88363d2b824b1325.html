Business Artiffact ()
A business artifact is a concrete, durable work product that records content, decisions, analyses, or deliverables produced by an Entoli agent within a value stream phase. It represents substantive output — such as a design, plan, report, code file, or specification — and serves as transferable value within the workspace. A business artifact is one of two subtypes of artifact, distinct from a normative artifact in that it conveys substance rather than prescribing rules or norms.
