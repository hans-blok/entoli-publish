Derivation Position ()
Derivation Position is the classification axis that specifies the positional relationship of a representation to the authoritative source. It records whether a representation is the leading (primary, authoritative) version or a derived (secondary, generated) copy. A leading representation is the source of truth; derived representations are produced from it and must remain consistent with it. POSITIONS DERIVATION POSITION - Leading — the primary, authoritative representation from which others are derived - Derived — a representation produced or generated from the leading representation
