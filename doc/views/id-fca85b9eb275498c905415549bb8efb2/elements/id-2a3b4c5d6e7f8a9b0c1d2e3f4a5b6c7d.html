An entoli-agent is an explicitly defined, autonomous software entity within the Entoli ecosystem that, on the basis of a formal agent-charter and agent-contract, executes specific tasks, produces Entoli artifacts or delivers information, and always operates within a demarcated agent-boundary and according to applicable governance.
