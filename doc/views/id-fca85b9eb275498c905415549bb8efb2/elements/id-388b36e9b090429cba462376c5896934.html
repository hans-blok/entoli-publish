## Conceptual Restructuring ### Definition Starting point: Concept Inventory. Purpose: from raw input → to coherent conceptual units. For data: - Abstracting concepts For work: - Clustering hypotheses into THEMEs ### Activities - Abstracting concrete terms into types - Generalising specific cases - Making implicit categories explicit - Resolving synonymy - Merging overlapping concepts - Eliminating redundancy - Sharpening scope - Distinguishing between type, role and instance ### What happens here Variation is reduced and meaning is lifted to a higher level of abstraction. This is the phase in which the concept field and the work are "cleaned up".
