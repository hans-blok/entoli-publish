We are sovereign ()
**Short description** We are sovereign: we make our own decisions and do not make ourselves structurally dependent on external parties, vendors or closed ecosystems. ### Rationale This principle safeguards that conviction at the organisational level and protects our independence and autonomy. By choosing sovereignty, we retain control over our own direction, knowledge and development. In a world where vendor lock-in and dependency on large platforms keep growing, this principle protects our freedom to choose what is best for our own situation. ### Implications **For decision-making**: - We deliberately choose open standards and open-source tools - In vendor choices, long-term autonomy weighs more heavily than short-term convenience - We build and maintain core knowledge ourselves rather than fully outsourcing it **For architecture and technology**: - Vendor lock-in is actively avoided - We prefer solutions where we fully control data and logic - We continue to develop and innovate ourselves, even when that requires more effort - We are investigating the possibility of developing our own LLM to retain full control over our AI capabilities **For organisation and culture**: - We invest in our own knowledge and skills - We document and share knowledge internally so we are not dependent on individual people or external parties - We take responsibility for our own course ### Examples **Good** ✓ - Using Linux as an operating system instead of proprietary alternatives - Choosing open-source databases with a strong community and standards - Developing and managing agents ourselves within Entoli rather than depending on closed-source AI platforms - Using Git with our own repository management instead of full dependency on a single cloud provider **Bad** ✗ - Going all-in on a single cloud provider without an exit strategy - Fully outsourcing critical business logic without retaining knowledge internally - Using proprietary formats with no conversion path to open standards - Becoming dependent on a single vendor for all tooling with no alternatives
